ACLU Challenge

Help create a tool to inform people of their rights when stopped by the police and make it more simple for them to file a complaint with the Milwaukee Fire & Police Commission.

Our Solution

We created an interphase that would collect the information needed for the Milwaukee Fire & Police Commission online form while also prompting additional questions to help the citizen form an effective testimonial. This form will also provide additional data points to create analytics. Example - following the lifecycle of the form etc.

Note: We will not save identifable information from the user for confidentiality purposes.

The Future

Our idea is to expand the interphase to include inputs into other government sites. Using our portal as a central location for submitting feedback improves user experience, saves time, and harvests data for analytics open to the public.
